All Divine Spells

Divine cantrips

  • Cantrip Chill Touch H (nec): Your touch hurts the living or disorients undead.
  • Cantrip Daze H (enc): Damage a creature’s mind and possibly stun it.
  • Cantrip Detect Magic H (div): Sense whether magic is nearby.
  • Cantrip Disrupt Undead H (nec): Damage undead with positive energy.
  • Cantrip Divine Lance H (evo): Throw divine energy that damages based on your deity’s alignment.
  • Cantrip Forbidding Ward H (abj): Protect an ally against one specific enemy.
  • Cantrip Guidance (div): Divine guidance improves one roll.
  • Cantrip Know Direction H (div): Find true north.
  • Cantrip Light H (evo): Make an object glow.
  • Cantrip Message H (ill): Speak a message to a distant creature, who can reply.
  • Cantrip Prestidigitation (evo): Perform a minor magical trick.
  • Cantrip Read Aura H (div): Detect if an object is magical, and determine the school of its magic.
  • Cantrip Shield H (abj): A shield of magical force blocks attacks and magic missiles.
  • Cantrip Sigil H (tra): Leave a magical mark.
  • Cantrip Stabilize (nec): Stabilize a dying creature.

Divine 1st-level Spells

  • Spell Air Bubble (con): React to create air for a creature to breathe.
  • Spell Alarm H (abj): Be alerted if a creature enters a warded area.
  • Spell Bane (enc): Weaken enemies’ attacks in an aura around you.
  • Spell Bless (enc): Strengthen allies’ attacks in an aura around you.
  • Spell Command H (enc): Bid a creature to approach, run, release something, lie prone, or stand up.
  • Spell Create Water (con): Conjure 2 gallons of water.
  • Spell Detect Alignment U, H (div): See auras of a chosen alignment.
  • Spell Detect Poison U, H (div): Determine whether an object or creature is poisonous or venomous.
  • Spell Disrupting Weapons H (nec): Weapons deal positive damage to undead.
  • Spell Fear H (enc): Frighten a creature, possibly making it flee.
  • Spell Harm H (nec): Negative energy harms the living or heals the undead, either a single creature or all in a burst.
  • Spell Heal H (nec): Positive energy heals the living or harms the undead, either a single creature or all in a burst.
  • Spell Lock H (abj): Make a lock hard to open.
  • Spell Magic Weapon (tra): Make a weapon temporarily magical.
  • Spell Mending H (tra): Repair one non-magical item.
  • Spell Protection U (abj): Shield a creature against those of a chosen alignment.
  • Spell Purify Food And Drink (nec): Make beverages and meals safe.
  • Spell Ray of Enfeeblement (nec): Sap a creature’s strength.
  • Spell Sanctuary (abj): Protect a creature from being attacked.
  • Spell Spirit Link H (nec): Continually transfer your health to someone else.
  • Spell Ventriloquism H (ill): Throw your voice.

Divine 2nd-level Spells

  • Spell Augury (div): Predict whether a course of action brings good fortune.
  • Spell Calm Emotions (enc): Suppress strong emotions and hostility.
  • Spell Comprehend Language H (div): A creature understands one language.
  • Spell Continual Flame H (evo): A magical flame burns indefinitely.
  • Spell Create Food H (con): Conjure food that can feed multiple creatures.
  • Spell Darkness H (evo): Suppress all light in an area.
  • Spell Darkvision H (div): See in the dark.
  • Spell Deafness (nec): Make a creature deaf.
  • Spell Death Knell (nec): Finish off a creature who’s near death.
  • Spell Dispel Magic (abj): End a spell or suppress an item’s magic.
  • Spell Endure Elements H (abj): Protect a creature from severe cold or heat.
  • Spell Enhance Victuals H (tra): Improve food or drink and remove poisons.
  • Spell Faerie Fire (evo): Colorful light prevents creatures from being concealed or invisible
  • Spell Gentle Repose H (nec): A corpse doesn’t decay and can’t become undead.
  • Spell Ghoulish Cravings (nec): Infect a creature with ghoul fever.
  • Spell Remove Fear H (enc): Free a creature from its fright.
  • Spell Remove Paralysis H (nec): Free a creature from paralysis.
  • Spell Resist Energy H (abj): Protect a creature from one type of energy damage.
  • Spell Restoration H (nec): Reduce a condition or lessen a toxin.
  • Spell Restore Senses H (nec): Remove a blinding or deafening effect.
  • Spell See Invisibility H (div): See invisible creatures and objects.
  • Spell Shield Other (Legacy) (nec): Absorb half the damage an ally would take.
  • Spell Silence H (ill): Mute all sound from a creature.
  • Spell Sound Burst H (evo): Damage and deafen creatures with a powerful din.
  • Spell Spiritual Weapon H (evo): Materialize a deific weapon of force that appears and attacks repeatedly.
  • Spell Status H (div): Keep track of a willing creature’s location and well-being.
  • Spell Undetectable Alignment U (abj): Make a creature or object appear neutral to alignment detection.
  • Spell Water Breathing H (tra): Allow creatures to breathe underwater.
  • Spell Water Walk H (tra): Buoy a creature so it can walk on water.

Divine 3rd-level Spells

  • Spell Bind Undead (nec): Take control of a mindless undead.
  • Spell Blindness (nec): Strike a target blind.
  • Spell Chilling Darkness H (evo): Ray of evil darkness deals cold damage, counteracts light, and harms celestials.
  • Spell Circle of Protection U, H (abj): A creature emits an aura that protects those within against an alignment.
  • Spell Crisis of Faith H (enc): Cause mental damage and possibly make a worshipper unable to cast spells.
  • Spell Dream Message H (enc): Send a message that arrives in a dream.
  • Spell Glyph of Warding (abj): Store a spell in a symbol to make a trap.
  • Spell Heroism H (enc): Stoke a creature’s inner heroism to make it more competent.
  • Spell Locate U, H (div): Learn the direction to an object.
  • Spell Neutralize Poison (nec): Cure a poison afflicting a creature.
  • Spell Remove Disease (nec): Cure a disease afflicting a creature.
  • Spell Sanctified Ground (abj): Create a 24-hour area of protection against aberrations, celestials, dragons, fiends, or undead.
  • Spell Searing Light H (evo): A ray of burning light deals extra damage to undead and counteracts darkness.
  • Spell Vampiric Touch H (nec): Deal negative damage and gain temporary HP with a touch.
  • Spell Wanderer’s Guide (div): Find an ideal route to a location.
  • Spell Zone of Truth U (enc): Compel creatures to tell the truth.

Divine 4th-level Spells

  • Spell Air Walk (tra): Walk on air as though it were solid ground.
  • Spell Anathematic Reprisal H (enc): Cause mental pain to one who commits anathema against your deity.
  • Spell Dimensional Anchor (abj): Keep a creature from teleporting or traveling to other planes.
  • Spell Discern Lies U (div): Expertly detect lies and falsehoods.
  • Spell Divine Wrath H (evo): Damage and hinder creatures of alignments opposed by your deity.
  • Spell Freedom of Movement (abj): A creature overcomes hindrances to its movement.
  • Spell Globe of Invulnerability U (abj): Magical sphere counteracts spells that would enter it.
  • Spell Holy Cascade H (evo): Turn a vial of holy water into an explosion of blessed water.
  • Spell Outcast’s Curse (enc): Curse a creature to be off-putting and grating.
  • Spell Read Omens U (div): Get a piece of advice about an upcoming event.
  • Spell Remove Curse (nec): Counteract a curse afflicting a creature.
  • Spell Spell Immunity (abj): Name a spell to negate its effects on you.
  • Spell Talking Corpse U (nec): Have a corpse answer three questions.
  • Spell Vital Beacon H (nec): Radiate vitality that heals creatures that touch you.

Divine 5th-level Spells

  • Spell Abyssal Plague (nec): Inflict a draining curse.
  • Spell Banishment H (abj): Send a creature back to its home plane.
  • Spell Breath of Life (nec): React to revive a creature at the moment of its death.
  • Spell Death Ward (abj): Protect a creature against negative energy.
  • Spell Drop Dead U, H (ill): The target appears to die but actually turns invisible.
  • Spell Flame Strike H (evo): Call divine fire from the sky.
  • Spell Prying Eye (div): An invisible eye transmits what it sees to you.
  • Spell Sending (div): Send a mental message to a creature anywhere on the planet and get a reply.
  • Spell Shadow Blast H (evo): Shape a cone of shadow to deal damage of a type you choose.
  • Spell Spiritual Guardian H (abj): Create a magical guardian to attack at your command and take damage for your allies.
  • Spell Summon Celestial H (con): Conjure a celestial to fight on your behalf.
  • Spell Summon Fiend H (con): Conjure a fiend to fight on your behalf.
  • Spell Tongues U, H (div): Let a creature understand and speak all languages.

Divine 6th-level Spells

  • Spell Blade Barrier H (evo): Form a wall of swords made of force.
  • Spell Field of Life H (nec): Create a positive energy field that heals those within.
  • Spell Raise Dead U, H (nec): Return a dead creature to life.
  • Spell Repulsion (abj): Prevent creatures from approaching you.
  • Spell Righteous Might H (tra): Turn into a battle form with divine armaments.
  • Spell Spellwrack (abj): Curse a creature to be harmed when a spell is cast on it and lower the duration of its spells.
  • Spell Spirit Blast H (nec): Damage a creature’s spiritual essence.
  • Spell Stone Tell U (div): Speak to spirits within natural stone.
  • Spell Stone to Flesh (tra): Turn a creature turned to stone back to flesh.
  • Spell True Seeing (div): See through illusions and transmutations.
  • Spell Vampiric Exsanguination H (nec): Draw blood and life force from creatures in a cone.
  • Spell Zealous Conviction H (enc): Instill unshakable conviction and zeal in willing creatures.

Divine 7th-level Spells

  • Spell Dimensional Lock U (abj): Prevent teleportation and planar travel.
  • Spell Divine Decree H (evo): Creatures of alignments opposed by your deity are damaged, enfeebled, paralyzed, or banished.
  • Spell Divine Vessel H (tra): Take on aspects of a servitor of your deity.
  • Spell Eclipse Burst H (nec): A globe of darkness deals cold damage, hurts the living, and overcomes light.
  • Spell Energy Aegis H (abj): A creature gains resistance to acid, cold, electricity, fire, force, and sonic.
  • Spell Ethereal Jaunt U, H (con): Use the Ethereal Plane to move through objects and into the air.
  • Spell Finger of Death H (nec): Point at a creature to deal negative damage and possibly kill it instantly.
  • Spell Plane Shift U (con): Transport creatures to another plane of existence.
  • Spell Regenerate H (nec): Cause a creature to heal over time, regrow organs, and reattach body parts.
  • Spell Sunburst H (evo): A globe of sunlight deals fire damage, hurts undead, and overcomes darkness.

Divine 8th-level Spells

  • Spell Antimagic Field R (abj): Magic doesn’t function in an area around you.
  • Spell Discern Location U (div): Discover a target’s exact location within unlimited range.
  • Spell Divine Aura (abj): Allies in an aura have better defenses and are protected against one alignment.
  • Spell Divine Inspiration (enc): Spiritual energy recovers a creature’s expended spell.
  • Spell Moment of Renewal (nec): Give a day’s recovery in an instant.
  • Spell Spiritual Epidemic (nec): Weaken a target with a communicable curse.

Divine 9th-level Spells

  • Spell Bind Soul U (nec): Imprison a dead creature’s soul.
  • Spell Crusade U, H (enc): Creatures become dedicated to a cause of your choice.
  • Spell Foresight (div): Sense when a creature is in danger and React to protect it with good fortune.
  • Spell Massacre H (nec): Instantly kill multiple creatures.
  • Spell Overwhelming Presence (enc): Take on the majesty of a god.
  • Spell Telepathic Demand (enc): Send a mental message that impels a creature toward a course of action.
  • Spell Wail of the Banshee (nec): Scream, dealing damage and draining creatures.
  • Spell Weapon of Judgment H (evo): Form a weapon to enforce war or peace.

Divine 10th-level Spells

  • Spell Avatar (tra): Transform into a battle form determined by your deity.
  • Spell Gate U (con): Tear open a portal to another plane.
  • Spell Miracle (div): Ask for a blessing to duplicate divine spells.
  • Spell Remake U (con): Recreate a destroyed object.
  • Spell Revival (nec): Heal creatures in an area and return the dead to life temporarily.
