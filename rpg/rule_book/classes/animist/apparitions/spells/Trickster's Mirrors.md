**Defense:** basic [[Will]]
**Duration:** sustained up to 1 minute 
___
You are surrounded by up to 3 mirrors that reflect twisted and distorted images of you, making it hard to tell where you actually are within your space and potentially causing those who attack you to hit one of the mirrors instead. You start with 1 mirror and gain an additional mirror each time you Sustain this spell, up to a maximum of 3 mirrors. Any attack that would hit you has a random chance of hitting one of your mirrors instead of you. With one mirror, the chances are 1in 2 (1-3 on 1d6). With two mirrors, there is a 1in 3 chance of hitting you (1-2 on 1d6). With three mirrors, there is a 1in 4 chance of hitting you (1 on 1d4). Once an image is hit, it is destroyed.
