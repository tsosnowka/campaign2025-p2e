**Harm**
**Level:** [[SPELL 1]]
[[HEALING]] | [[MANIPULATE]] | [[VITALITY]]
**Traditions**: [[divine]], [[primal]]
**Cast:** [![](action_one.png)](one-action),[![](action_two.png)](two-actions),[![](action_three.png)](three-actions)
**Range:** varies
**Targets:** 1 willing living creature or 1 undead creature
___
You channel vital energy to heal the living or damage the undead. If the target is a willing living creature, you restore 1d8 Hit Points. If the target is undead, you deal that amount of vitality damage to it, and it gets a basic Fortitude save. The number of actions you spend when Casting this Spell determines its targets, range, area, and other parameters.

[![](action_one.png)](one-action) The spell has a range of touch.
[![](action_two.png)](two-actions) ([[concentrate]]) The spell has a range of 30 feet. If you're healing a living creature, increase the Hit Points restored by 8.
[![](action_three.png)](three-actions) ([[concentrate]]) You disperse vital energy in a 30-foot emanation. This targets all living and undead creatures in the burst.
