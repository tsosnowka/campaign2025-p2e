**Create Food**
**Level:** [[SPELL 2]]
[[CONCENTRATE]] | [[MANIPULATE]]
**Traditions**: [[arcane]], [[divine]], [[primal]]
**Cast:** [[long-length-action]] 1 hour
**Range:** 30 feet
___
You create enough food to feed 6 Medium creatures for a day. This food is bland and unappealing, but it is nourishing. After 1 day, if no one has eaten the food, it decays and becomes inedible. Most Small creatures eat one-quarter as much as a Medium creature (one-sixteenth as much for most Tiny creatures), and most Large creatures eat 10 times as much (100 times as much for Huge creatures and so on).
___
- **Heightened (4th)** You can feed 12 Medium creatures.  
- **Heightened (6th)** You can feed 50 Medium creatures.  
- **Heightened (8th)** You can feed 200 Medium creatures.
