**Gouging Claw**
**Level:** [[CANTRIP 1]]
[[ATTACK]] | [[CONCENTRATE]] | [[MANIPULATE]] | [[MORPH]] | [[CANTRIP]]
**Traditions**: [[arcane]], [[primal]]
**Cast:** [![](action_two.png)](two-actions) 
**Range:** touch
**Targets:** 1 creature
**Defense:** [[AC]]
___
You temporarily morph your limb into a clawed appendage. Make a melee spell attack roll against your target's AC. If you hit, you deal your choice of **3**d6 slashing damage or **3**d6 piercing damage, plus **3** persistent bleed damage. On a critical success, you deal double damage and double bleed damage.  
___
- **Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1d6 and the persistent bleed damage increases by 1.  
  
_Auto-heightened from 1st-rank (+1), gaining new benefits at 2nd-rank._
